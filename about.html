About Bethany

We would love to meet up and chat about how we can make your dream wedding happen!

Bethany Wedding is a wedding dress photography institute that takes quality as its principle, committed to bringing satisfaction to customers with high-class service, at affordable prices. Appreciate advanced technology and techniques in the world to create a distinct photography style. With the service motto: Friendly, enthusiastic, thoughtful and quality as the top priority, Bethany is always committed to bringing the best values to her customers in particular and to the wedding industry in general!

Bethany Wedding today has become a familiar name, a place frequented by young couples who want to mark the beautiful memories of their lives. Besides, Bethany also thrives in other artistic photography fields such as family photography, pregnancy photography, baby photography.
